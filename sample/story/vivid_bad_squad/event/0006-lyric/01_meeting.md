# 1. A Voice in the Crowd

Kohane: I heard her voice at the street corner, and my feet just stopped moving.
An: You came back. I saw you in the crowd yesterday too.
Kohane: I don't know how to sing like you. I get scared when people look at me.
An: Then start by singing for one person. Sing for me.

---

Kohane: My name is Kohane Azusawa. I want to learn how to put my true feelings into a song.
An: I'm An Shiraishi. If you're serious, meet me at the Vivid Street stage tonight.
Kohane: Tonight. I'll be there. I promise I won't run away this time.
