# 1. Stray Bad Dog

Akito: Everyone expects me to be the confident one. They don't see how much I doubt myself.
Toya: I've known you since we were kids. You don't have to carry it alone.
Akito: When did you get so sentimental, Toya?
Toya: Since I realized winning means nothing if I lose the people beside me.

---

Akito: Fine. Let's win the next battle together, back to back, like always.
Toya: Back to back. That's the only way I know how to fight.
